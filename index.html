What is
Nostalgia.exe?

A web-based gaming hub that blends retro computing aesthetics with futuristic cyber visuals. We evoke emotional recall of early digital culture — Windows 95, VHS tapes, cassettes, pixel UI — through a modern, fluid, interactive interface.

It's a bridge between past imagination and future technology: the future as it was dreamed in 1989, rebuilt with today's tools.

#retrofuturism #crt #pixelart #vaporwave #cyberpunk #indie
